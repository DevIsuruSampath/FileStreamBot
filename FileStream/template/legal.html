Quick Summary

This bot turns Telegram uploads into shareable streaming and download pages. To do that, it stores link metadata, Telegram identifiers, moderation records, and short-lived web access tokens.

Important Rules

Do not upload illegal, infringing, or adult content. Links may stop working if the backing Telegram/FLOG file is removed, revoked, or expired.

Need Help?

Use the bot for the latest commands, or contact the admin inside Telegram if you need moderation or privacy support.
